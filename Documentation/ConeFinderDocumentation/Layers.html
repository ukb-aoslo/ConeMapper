Layers

Almost all option of data presentation in Cone Finder app can be combined.
In this article it will be explained what data lies on which layer.
  1. The first layer is image itself. The image filters and brightness settings will be applied to this layer.
  2. The second layer is density map. It is shown transparent by default, but user can make in non-transparent.
  3. The third layer is Voronoi diagram.
  4. The fourth layer is the cone marks.
  5. The fifth layer is the density contour lines.
  6. The sixth layer is the PCD marker.
  7. The seventh layer is the CDC marker.
  8. The eighth layer is the scalebar.
  9. The nineth layer is the grid
